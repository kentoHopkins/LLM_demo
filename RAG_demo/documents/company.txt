NovaMind AI — Company Overview
================================

Company Name: NovaMind AI
Founded: 2021
Headquarters: Austin, Texas, USA
CEO: Dr. Priya Anand
CTO: Marcus Leung
Total Employees: 87
Funding Stage: Series B ($42M raised)

Mission
-------
NovaMind AI's mission is to make intelligent automation accessible to small and mid-sized businesses that lack the engineering resources of large enterprises. We believe every business deserves AI that actually works for them.

What We Do
----------
NovaMind AI builds a no-code AI workflow platform called FlowMind. FlowMind allows non-technical users to create, deploy, and manage AI-powered automations using a drag-and-drop interface. Our platform connects to over 200 third-party services including Slack, Salesforce, Notion, QuickBooks, and Shopify.

Core Products
-------------
1. FlowMind Studio — the main drag-and-drop automation builder
2. FlowMind Insights — an analytics dashboard showing automation performance
3. FlowMind Copilot — an AI assistant embedded inside FlowMind Studio that suggests workflow improvements

Key Customers
-------------
NovaMind AI currently serves over 1,400 businesses across 34 countries. Notable customers include Brightleaf Logistics, Sunvera Health, and Pebble Street Media. Our average customer saves 14 hours per week through FlowMind automations.
